KaizoCore — Frontend examples

FORGE — Checkout (await settle before submitting)

Use FORGE for: checkout, login, signup, payment, OTP verify, coupon apply. The ~600ms challenge runs in the background while the user fills out the form. By the time they click "Place Order", settle() resolves instantly.

FORGE — Login

AMBIENT — Search (no settle needed)

For AMBIENT mode, no settle() call is needed. The SDK collects passive signals in the background and the kz_st cookie is set within ~50ms of page load. Just make your fetch — the cookie is already there.

PULSE — Ticket booking (require heartbeats)

PULSE emits a heartbeat every 4s. By requiring minHeartbeats: 3 before booking, you ensure the user was genuinely present for ~12 seconds — filtering bots that joined the queue programmatically at open.
